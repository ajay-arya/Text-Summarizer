In 1874 Ernst Haeckel divided the animal kingdom into the multicellular Metazoa now synonymous with Animalia and the Protozoa single celled organisms no longer considered animals. Carl Linnaeus cre ated the first hierarchical biological classification for animals in 1758 with his S ystema Naturae which Jean Baptiste Lamarck expanded into 14 phyla by 1809. Animals range in length from 8.5 millionths of a metre to 33.6 metres 110 ft and have complex interactions with each other and their environments forming intricate food webs .
